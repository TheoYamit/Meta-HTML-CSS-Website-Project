Who are we?

We are a team based in Madison, Wisconsin. We are a boutique hair salon that specialized in cut, color and styling. We offer makeup and nail services as well, and we pride ourselves in our relaxing environment. Clients need to book in advance.

Our Team

Pria, the owner of Hair Day, earned her certificate to be a colorist and started to specialize in custom coloring techniques that many people adored. Opening Hair Day with her friend Garry, they sought to make a "treat yourself" environment for their clients. Currently, Hair Day has seven staff, two sylists, three colorists, one manicurist, and one make-up artist.
